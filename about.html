About Us

Who We Are

The game Rust was developed by Facepunch Studios, founded by Garry Newman in 2004. They initially gained popularity with the game Garry's Mod. Rust was released in early access in 2013 and has become one of the most successful multiplayer survival games.

The developers continuously add new content and improve gameplay, supporting an active player community. Garry Newman and his team focus heavily on the technical aspects of the game, ensuring high performance and server stability.
